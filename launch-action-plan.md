### DAY 1 — You set up Calendly (30 min)

**What you do:**
1. Go to calendly.com and create a free account using your MTN BUFF email
2. Create one event type: **"Free 15-Min Buyer Strategy Call"**
3. Set your available times (suggest 3 slots per day, Mon–Fri)
4. Copy your Calendly link — it'll look like `calendly.com/padenanderson/15min`
5. Send that link to Ethan

**What Ethan does after:**
- Updates the "Book a Free 15-Min Call" button on the live page to point to your Calendly link
- Pushes the update live (takes 5 minutes)

---

### DAY 2 — You set up the Jotform capture form (45 min)

**What you do:**
1. Go to jotform.com and create a free account
2. Create a new form — two fields only:
   - **First Name**
   - **Email Address**
3. Set the form's "Thank You" page to redirect to the live guide URL (your Vercel link) after someone submits — this is in Form Settings → Thank You Page → Redirect to URL
4. Style the form to match MTN BUFF colors if desired (burnt orange `#b53c12`, Poppins font)
5. **Critical step:** Go to Settings → Emails in Jotform. Add a notification email that fires to your **Follow Up Boss lead intake email address** every time someone submits. (See below for how to find that address in FUB.)

**How to find your FUB lead intake email:**
- Log into Follow Up Boss
- Go to Admin → Lead Sources → Email Parsing
- Your FUB intake address will look like something like `leads@youraccount.followupboss.com`
- Copy it and paste it as the recipient of the Jotform notification email

That's it. When someone submits the form → Jotform fires an email → FUB receives it → contact is automatically created in FUB.

### DAY 4 — You build a simple landing page (45 min)

The Vercel page is the guide itself. You need a separate page where people land *before* they get the guide — this is where they enter their email.

**Two options (pick one):**

**Option A — Use Jotform's built-in landing page (easiest)**
Jotform lets you publish the form as a standalone page with a URL. Customize it with:
- Headline: *"Get the Free Guide: 7 Things You Can Negotiate Right Now"*
- 3 bullet points of what's inside
- The 2-field form

**Option B — Use a page on mtnbuff.com (better for brand)**
If the MTN BUFF website is on WordPress or Squarespace, add a new page at `mtnbuff.com/playbook` and embed the Jotform on it. This looks more professional and keeps traffic on your own domain.

**What Ethan does after:**
- Updates the "Book a Free 15-Min Call" button throughout the guide page if any links need updating
