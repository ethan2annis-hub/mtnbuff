THE RATE BUYDOWN

[BODY — Poppins 14px]
Ask the seller to pay cash at closing to permanently lower your interest rate — or to cover the first two years at a reduced rate (called a "2-1 buydown").

[DOLLAR VALUE CALLOUT — terracotta #d47f2f background, white text, Poppins bold]
💰 Worth up to $400/month in year one on a $400K home.
That's $4,800 you keep in year one alone.

[SMALL ITALIC — muted gray]
*In 2022: Sellers laughed at this. Now it's a standard ask.*

---

[CARD 2]
[NUMBER: large, burnt orange] 02

[CARD HEADER — Playfair Display ALL CAPS]
CLOSING COST CREDITS

[BODY — Poppins 14px]
Ask the seller to cover some or all of your closing costs at the table. Closing costs typically run 2–5% of the purchase price — money you'd otherwise bring out of pocket on top of your down payment.

[DOLLAR VALUE CALLOUT — terracotta #d47f2f background]
💰 On a $400K home: up to $20,000 you don't have to bring to closing.

[SMALL ITALIC — muted gray]
*This is now one of the most common concessions in Northern Utah.*

---

[CARD 3]
[NUMBER: large, burnt orange] 03

[CARD HEADER — Playfair Display ALL CAPS]
INSPECTION REPAIRS & CREDITS

[BODY — Poppins 14px]
You can now inspect the home AND negotiate. After inspection, you can ask for a repair credit (cash at closing), require the seller to fix specific issues, or use findings to renegotiate the price.

[DOLLAR VALUE CALLOUT — terracotta #d47f2f background]
💰 Inspection credits commonly run $2,000–$10,000+ depending on what's found.

[SMALL ITALIC — muted gray]
*In 2022: Buyers waived inspections entirely just to compete. That era is over.*

[CARD 4]
[NUMBER: large, burnt orange] 04

[CARD HEADER — Playfair Display ALL CAPS]
POST-APPRAISAL PRICE REDUCTION

[BODY — Poppins 14px]
If the home appraises below your offer price, you can now negotiate the purchase price down to the appraised value — or walk away with your earnest money intact.

[DOLLAR VALUE CALLOUT — terracotta #d47f2f background]
💰 In 2022, buyers routinely paid $20K–$50K over appraisal. You don't have to do that anymore.

[SMALL ITALIC — muted gray]
*Sellers understand this. Most would rather adjust than restart.*
